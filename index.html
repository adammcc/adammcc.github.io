Rynoq Charts

Rynoq charts visualizes and animates stock volatility of individual stocks since the year 2000 or their IPO. Rynoq features both a single stock view page and a comparison (battle) view page.

Technology used: Ruby, Rails 4, Javascript, mongoDB, d3.js, backbone.js, Yahoo Finance's API

edPeople

edPeople 1.0 is a locally-focused professional networking platform for K-12 educators with a vision of retaining good teachers in NYC's schools. This app will allow educators to connect with colleagues, discover job opportunities and actively manage their careers. This site is still under construction. Visit the beta site at edpeople.com.

Technology used: Ruby on Rails, postgreSQL, Linkedin's API and Geocoder.

Flosswords

Flosswords is an infinite photo crossword puzzle that contains words scraped from New York Times headlines and picture clues from Flickr. Instead of word clues, Flossowrds displays pictures that are associated with each crossword answer. Gameplay is controlled fully within the player's browser.

Tecnology used: Ruby, Ruby on rails, Javascript, postgreSQL, AJAX, Nokogiri, Flickr's API

Crowded Lands

Crowded Lands visualizes the population density of every country in the world. Simply enter a country for a visual representation of the population density of one square mile.

Technology used: Ruby, Rails 4, Javascript, mongoDB, d3.js, and Nokogiri
